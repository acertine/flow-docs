---
title: Understanding Financial Reports
description: Learn how to access and use financial reports in Acertine Flow to track revenue, payments, and outstanding invoices.
---

## Available Reports

### Revenue Reports

- Total revenue by period
- Paid invoices summary
- Revenue trends
- Monthly/yearly totals

### Outstanding Invoices

- Unpaid invoices list
- Overdue invoices
- Total outstanding
- By client breakdown

### Payment Reports

- Payment history
- Payment methods
- Payment trends
- Collection rates

## Using Reports

### Accessing Reports

1. Go to **Reporting** page
2. Select report type
3. Choose date range
4. View results

### Exporting Data

- Export to CSV
- Download PDF reports
- Share with accountant
- Keep records

## Report Features

### Date Ranges

- This month
- Last month
- This quarter
- This year
- Custom range

### Filters

- Filter by client
- Filter by status
- Filter by date
- Multiple filters

## Best Practices

- Review reports regularly
- Export for accounting
- Track trends
- Identify issues

Financial reports help you understand your business performance!
