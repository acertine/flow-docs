## Profile Information

### Personal Details

- **Full Name:** Your name
- **Email:** Account email (from Clerk)
- **Phone:** Contact number
- **Website:** Personal or business website

### Profile Picture

- Upload profile photo
- Used in account
- Visible to you only
- Can be updated anytime

## Updating Profile

1. Go to **Settings > Profile**
2. Edit fields as needed
3. Save changes
4. Updates apply immediately

## Best Practices

- Keep information current
- Use professional photo
- Include business website
- Update contact details

Your profile helps personalise your Flow experience!
