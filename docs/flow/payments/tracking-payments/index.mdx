## Payment Status Overview

### Dashboard View

- Paid invoices count
- Pending payments
- Overdue amounts
- Total outstanding

### Invoice Status

- **Unpaid:** Awaiting payment
- **Paid:** Payment received
- **Overdue:** Past due date
- **Partial:** Partially paid

## Manual Payment Tracking

### Mark as Paid

1. Open invoice
2. Click **Mark as Paid**
3. Enter payment date
4. Add notes (optional)
5. Save

### Payment Details

- Payment date
- Payment method
- Amount received
- Reference number

## Automatic Tracking

### Stripe Payments

### Payment Notifications

- Email when paid

- Status changes
- Payment summaries

## Best Practices

### Tracking

- Mark payments promptly
- Verify payment amounts
- Record payment methods
- Keep detailed notes

### Follow-up

- Monitor overdue invoices
- Send reminders regularly
- Follow up personally
- Document communications

### Reporting

- Review reports regularly
- Identify payment trends
- Address slow payers
- Improve cash flow
