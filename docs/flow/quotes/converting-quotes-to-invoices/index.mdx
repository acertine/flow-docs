---

## When to Convert

Convert quotes when:

- Client accepts the quote
- Work is confirmed
- Ready to invoice
- Starting the project

## How to Convert

### Method 1: From Quote Page

1. Open the quote
2. Click Convert to Invoice
3. Review converted invoice
4. Make any adjustments
5. Save or send

### Method 2: From Quotes List

1. Go to Quotes page
2. Find approved quote
3. Click Convert button
4. Confirm conversion

## What Gets Converted

### Preserved Details

- All line items
- Client information
- Descriptions
- VAT rates
- Payment terms
- Notes

### New Information

- New invoice number
- Current issue date
- New due date
- Invoice status

## After Conversion

### Quote Status

- Original quote marked as accepted
- Shows conversion history
- Maintains link to invoice

### Invoice Creation

- New invoice created
- Ready to send
- Can be edited
- Tracks separately

## Best Practices

- Convert promptly after approval
- Review before sending
- Adjust dates if needed
- Update amounts if changed
- Send invoice immediately

Converting quotes streamlines your workflow!
